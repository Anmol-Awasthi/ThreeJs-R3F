The Sun

Our stellar neighbor, a massive ball of hydrogen and helium, burning at 27 million degrees Fahrenheit at its core. The Sun's powerful gravity holds the entire solar system together.

The Earth

Our home planet, the only known world harboring life. Wrapped in clouds and oceans, Earth spins on its axis while completing its yearly dance around the Sun.

The Moon

Earth's faithful companion, controlling our tides and lighting up our nights. The Moon completes its orbit around Earth every 27.3 days, always showing us the same face.
